Create an Incident

1
Go to INCIDENTS in sidebar
2
Tap + NEW INCIDENT
3
Select Type, fill Title
4
Pick Zone and Service if known
5
Tap SUBMIT INCIDENT
Offline? Tap submit anyway — saves as local draft automatically.

Add a Note to Incident

1
Open the incident from INCIDENTS list
2
Scroll to TIMELINE at bottom
3
Type your update in the text box
4
Tap ADD
Notes are permanent. They cannot be edited or deleted.

Log an Operation Entry

1
Go to OPERATIONS in sidebar
2
Tap + LOG OPERATION
3
Select your Service
4
Add Notes if needed
5
Tap SUBMIT
Operations require an active shift. If you see "No active shift" — contact your manager.

When Offline

1
Topbar shows OFFLINE
2
Keep working — drafts save locally
3
When back online — tap DRAFTS in sidebar
4
Verify all drafts show SYNCED
5
If any show REJECTED — tap RETRY

Incident Status Meanings

ACTIVE Incident is open and being handled. Add notes to update the timeline.
ESCALATED Incident requires manager attention. Visible at top of NOW screen.
RESOLVED Incident closed by a manager. No further actions possible.
DRAFT Incident saved offline. Will sync automatically when connection restores.
