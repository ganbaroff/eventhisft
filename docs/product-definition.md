# OPSBOARD — Product Definition

## What OPSBOARD is

OPSBOARD is an **adaptive operations platform for live events and operational departments**.

It provides operational visibility during live event time, supports fast and honest capture of incidents and routine operational entries, and preserves accountability with a full audit trail.

**First real-world use case:** WUF13 / Guest Services department.

## Core purpose

- Provide operational visibility during live event time
- Allow fast and honest capture of incidents and routine operational entries
- Support coordination under pressure
- Preserve accountability and audit trail
- Tolerate unstable internet conditions
- Remain simple in the field

## What success looks like

- A coordinator can capture incidents or routine operational state fast and honestly
- A manager can open the system and understand what needs attention in under 10 seconds
- The system becomes more reliable than fragmented WhatsApp or chat-based coordination

## Primary users (MVP)

- **Coordinators** — primary field users
- **Senior Managers** — secondary oversight
- **Service Managers** — secondary operational review

Volunteers are NOT primary users in MVP.

## Platform

Tablet-first responsive web app / PWA.
Works on tablet, laptop, browser-based internal environments.
Not a native mobile app.
