# OPSBOARD — Non-Goals

These are permanent exclusions. Do not build these. Do not drift toward these.

## Analytics & Reporting
- No trend analytics in MVP
- No advanced BI dashboards
- No charts in NOW screen
- No historical comparisons
- No KPI engine
- No cross-shift intelligence

## Configuration & Workflow
- No workflow builder
- No advanced alert automation engine
- No generic configurator platform
- No no-code platform builder
- No advanced permission builder

## Data Integrity Anti-patterns
- No hardcoded operational numbers (staff, volunteer, zone counts)
- No destructive history rewrites
- No deleting incidents
- No silent conflict resolution
- No fake sync certainty

## Scope Creep
- No speculative v2 abstractions in MVP
- No role explosion
- No bulk incident actions
- No offline resolve/escalate/admin

## Wrong Product Category
- Not HR software
- Not workforce planning software
- Not payroll system
- Not attendee registration platform
- Not generic task manager
- Not enterprise ERP
- Not universal workflow builder
